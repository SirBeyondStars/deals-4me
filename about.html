About Deals-4Me

Deals-4Me helps families save money with weekly circulars, side-by-side price comparisons, a simple wallet, and a few fun learning games. We’re building this openly, one “room” at a time.

What we do

  • Flyers Hub: browse weekly store flyers in one place and compare overlapping items to spot the best price.
  • Wallet: track purchases and see savings; Receipts live under Wallet.
  • FLPs (Family Life Points): planned rewards for helpful actions (e.g., validated receipts).
  • Games: quick kid-friendly learning (memory, plant builder, quick math).
  • Studio (coming later): paint-by-numbers with real art and artist support.

How it works (today)

Phase 3 focuses on a fast, clean front-end. Flyers use a simple data feed; Wallet and Receipts keep data locally in your browser. We’ll add secure accounts and syncing next.

Roadmap (next)

  • Receipt OCR + FLP rewards
  • Store coverage expansion & better item matching
  • Optional accounts (sync across devices)
  • Artist’s Studio (levels & monetization)

FAQ

Is Deals-4Me free?

Yes for the basics (flyers, games, wallet). We may add optional paid tiers later for power features.

How do the flyer comparisons work?

You can select any number of flyers. We line up items that appear in 2+ of the selected flyers and highlight the lowest price. Data is demo today and will expand as we add sources.

What are FLPs (Family Life Points)?

FLPs are points for helpful actions (e.g., validated receipts) that unlock perks later. In Phase 3, they’re placeholders.

Do you scan receipts?

Receipt upload is planned. You’ll be able to submit a photo; we’ll parse store/date/items and award FLPs. Everything stays private—only aggregated, anonymized pricing is used to improve comparisons.

Do you partner with stores?

Not required to start. We’ll grow via public flyers and community submissions, then add partnerships as the audience grows.

Is my data private?

Yes. Today, Wallet/Receipts data is stored locally in your browser. When accounts arrive, we’ll document exactly what’s stored and why.
