Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Deals-4Me is designed to minimize personal data collection, especially during our early phases. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

What we collect (Phase 3)

  • Basic usage: No account is required. Circulars, games, and wallet work without signup.
  • Local storage: Wallet, receipts, and shopping lists save in your browser only. We can’t see them.
  • Feedback forms: Suggestions/Contact forms send your input to our team email (via our provider).
  • Analytics: We may add anonymous usage analytics (page visits, clicks) to improve navigation.

Future phases

When accounts launch, we’ll collect only the data needed to sync your lists, FLPs, and preferences. We’ll update this page before that goes live.

Your choices

  • You can clear Wallet/Receipts data any time (browser settings).
  • You can submit feedback anonymously (no login needed).
  • If you create an account later, you’ll be able to delete it at any time.

Terms of Service

By using Deals-4Me, you agree to the following simple terms. We keep these short and plain-language for now.

Use of the service

  • Deals-4Me is for personal, non-commercial use. Don’t resell or scrape our data without permission.
  • Games, flyers, and wallet tools are provided “as-is” — accuracy isn’t guaranteed yet.

Rewards (FLPs)

  • Family Life Points (FLPs) are experimental and may change or reset as the system evolves.
  • No cash value at this stage; later perks will be defined in writing.

User contributions

  • If you submit prices or receipts, you give us permission to anonymize and aggregate them to improve comparisons.
  • You remain the owner of your own purchase data — we only use it with your consent.

Liability

We’re not responsible if you miss a sale or a price changes after being listed. Always double-check at the store.
