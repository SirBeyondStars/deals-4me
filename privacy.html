Deals-4Me Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

1. What We Collect

We collect personal data such as your email, 4-digit PIN, zip code, shopping preferences, and saved circulars. Pro users may optionally upload a profile photo.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To customize and improve your Deals‑4Me experience
  • To suggest relevant deals and local store flyers
  • To offer gamified savings and budgeting tools
  • To manage account tiers, subscriptions, and upgrades

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and Firebase Analytics to understand user behavior, track trends, and improve our features.

4. Ad Personalization

Ads in the free tier (Basic) may be personalized using anonymized data, but we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Integrations

We use third-party services like Stripe for payments, Firebase for authentication, and Flipp (or similar) for flyer integration. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request full deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Deals‑4Me does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. Kids Mode features are family-supervised only.

8. Updates to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted here. Continued use of Deals‑4Me after changes means you agree to the new terms.
