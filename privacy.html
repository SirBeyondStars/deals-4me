Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Deals-4Me (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services (the “Service”).

1) Information We Collect

  • Account info: email, password (hashed), subscription tier, household settings.
  • Household PIN: used for quick authentication by members of a household.
    Note: In production, we never store your PIN in plaintext. The demo UI may use localStorage on your device for testing only.
  • Usage: pages visited, searches (e.g., ZIP + radius), saved items count, device/browser metadata.
  • Support: messages you send via contact forms or email.
  • Payment: handled by our payment processor (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (Deals Hub search, saved items, flyers, recipes).
  • Manage your subscription and account access (including household PIN).
  • Improve performance, reliability, and usability of the Service.
  • Personalize content (e.g., remembering your last ZIP or curbside preference).
  • Communicate with you (service updates, billing, customer support).
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse.

3) Cookies & Local Storage

We use a combination of cookies and browser storage to keep the Service running smoothly.

Name / KeyTypePurposeExpires
session Cookie Keep you signed in securely. Session / up to 30 days
d4m_search localStorage Remembers last ZIP & radius in the Hub. Until you clear it
d4m_pin_* localStorage (demo) Demo-only: stores PIN locally during testing. Not used in production. Until you clear it
Analytics cookie(s) Cookie Anonymous usage metrics (page performance, feature usage). Varies (typically 1–12 months)

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Blocking required cookies may impact site functionality.

4) When We Share Information

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, email, payments (e.g., Stripe). They process data on our behalf under contracts.
  • Sponsors / Partners: if you interact with a partner signup or referral link, we may share limited data needed to fulfill the request.
  • Legal: to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: during mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, data may be transferred as permitted by law.

We do not sell your personal information.

5) Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information (encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls). The household PIN is treated like a short passcode—in production it is never stored in plaintext. The demo on your device using localStorage is for testing only.

6) Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account data (see “Your Privacy Rights”).

7) Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, download, or delete your personal information, and to object or restrict certain processing. To make a request, contact us (see below). We will verify your identity and respond as required by applicable law.

  • Do Not Track: We do not respond to DNT signals at this time.
  • Marketing emails: You can unsubscribe via the link in the email.

8) Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us to request deletion.

9) International Users

If you access the Service from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice (e.g., by email or in-product). Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective signifies acceptance.
