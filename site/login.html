How it works

Deals-4Me helps you track the groceries you actually buy and quickly spot what’s worth grabbing this week — without being a marketplace or a social app.

  • Pick your stores (only the ones you shop)
  • Save your staples (eggs, chicken, cereal, produce)
  • See your deals fast and compare prices in one place

Coverage grows over time as we add more stores.
