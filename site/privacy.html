How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers (such as email, hosting, or payment processors) when required to operate the service.

SMS Alerts

If you opt into text alerts, messages are sent solely to notify you of relevant deals or account activity. The SMS number used is automated and not monitored for support requests.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Deals-4Me is designed for household use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental involvement.
