Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are billed monthly and you can cancel whenever you like. Your plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle.

How secure are payments?

All payments are handled through Stripe. Deals-4Me never stores your credit card information.

Do saved item limits apply per person?

No. Limits are at the household level. For example, Gold includes 40 total saved items shared across family members using their PINs.

What if I hit my save limit?

Delete older saved items to free space, or upgrade to a higher plan for more room.
